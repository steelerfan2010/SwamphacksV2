Powerball Analysis Tools and Predictor

What’s a powerball?

The powerball is a lottery where you choose 6 numbers. The chances of winning anything at all are about 1 in 25. The chances of winning the jackpot are about 1 in 292,200,000.

What is this?

It is a website with a set of tools to help you understand the powerball, analyze its past data, and help you choose your numbers.

How do I win?

One out of a set of prizes is awarded based on how many numbers you match to the numbers that are drawn. Here is a visual.

How do I pick my numbers?

At the bottom of this page, you have two options. You can play the numbers generated by our algorithm, or we also generate random numbers. If you don’t want to trust us, you can also use our tools to make your own decision.

Algorithm? What’s that?

We have our own formula to choose what we believe are the most likely numbers to be chosen. We use the past drawings in the current system and give preference to numbers that appeared the most.

What are these ‘system’ things?

Over the history of the powerball, the possible numbers of the white balls, and the powerball changed. Here is a chart of the possible numbers for each system.

Why?

Because each time you play, you ask yourself the same question. What are the best numbers I can choose? This is our best attempt to answer that question with the click of a button.
